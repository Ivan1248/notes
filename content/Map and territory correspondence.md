In the **map and territory correspondence** metaphor:
- **territory** corresponds to for [[The world|the world]] or [[Fact|facts]], or "what is really there".
- **map** corresponds to a set of [[Statement|statements]] or [[Belief|beliefs]] about the world,

## Notes

- The purpose of the metaphor is to illustrate that representations do not completely and without error correspond to what they represent.
- From [The Useful Idea of Truth — LessWrong](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/XqvnWFtRD2keJdwjX/the-useful-idea-of-truth):
  > \[...\] _beliefs_ determine my experimental _predictions,_ but only _reality_ gets to determine my experimental _results_.
