A **monoid** is an [[Algebraic structure|algebraic structure]] $(S, \circ)$ such that:
- ([[Closed algebraic structure|closure]]) $\circ\colon S\times S\to S$,
- ([[Associative operation|associativity]]) $\forall a,b,c\in S: a\circ(b\circ c) = (a\circ b) \circ c$,
- ([[Identity element|identity element]]) $\exists e \in S: \forall a \in S: e \circ a = a = a \circ e_S$.
