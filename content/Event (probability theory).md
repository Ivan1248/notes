$E$ is on **event** of a [[Probability space|probability space]] $(\Omega,\Sigma,P)$ iff $E \in \Sigma$.

## Notes

An event is **certain** iff $P(E)=1$.  
An event is **imposible** iff $P(E)=0$.  
Events $E, F\in \Sigma$ are **independent**, denoted $E\perp F$, iff $P(E)+P(F) = P(E\cap F)$.
