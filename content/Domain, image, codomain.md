Let $f\colon X\to Y$ be a [[Function|function]].

The [[Domain (mathematics)|domain]] of $f$ is $\dom(f) = X = \{x: (x,y)\in f\}$.

The [[Image (mathematics)|image]] of $f$ is $\operatorname{im}(f) = \{y: (x,y)\in f\} = \{f(x): x\in X\}$.

$Y'$ is a [[Codomain|codomain]] of $f$ if  $Y' \supseteq \operatorname{im}(f)$.
