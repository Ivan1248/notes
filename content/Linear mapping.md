Let $X$ and $Y$ be [[Vector space|vector spaces]] over a [[Field (Math))|field]] $R$.

A **linear mapping** is a [[Function|function]] $f\colon X\to Y$ such that for all $\v x, \v v\in X$:
- $f(a \v x) = a f(\v x)$ ([[Additivity|additivity]]),
- $f(\v x+\v v) = f(\v x) + f(\v v)$ ([[Homogeneity|homogeneity]]).
