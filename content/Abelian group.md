Let $G$ be a [[Set|set]].

An **abelian group** is $(G, +)$ where $+ : G \times G \to G$ and:
- (associativity) $\forall x,y,z\in G:\ (x + y) + z = x + (y + z)$,
- (commutativity) $\forall x,y\in G:\ x + y = y + x$,
- (identity) $\exists 0_G \in G\ \forall x\in G:\ x + 0_G = x$,
- (inverse) $\forall x\in G\ \exists -x \in G:\ x + (-x) = 0_G$.
