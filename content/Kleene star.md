Synonyms: Kleene star.

$S^*$ is the set of all [[Sequence|sequences]] over $S$:
$$
S^* = \bigcup_{n\in \N_0} S^n \text.
$$

## Alternative definition

$S^*$ is the set of all [[Sequence|sequences]] over $S$:
$$
S^* = \{()\} \cup\{(x_1, x_2, .., x_n) : n\in \N_0 \land \forall i\in\{1\bidot n\}\colon x_i\in S\} \text.
$$

## Notes

- $()$ is the empty sequence.
- $\N$ is [[Natural numbers|the set of natural numbers]].
