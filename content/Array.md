Let $S$ be a [[Set|set]].  
Let $d_1, .., d_n \in \N_{\geq 1}$.  

An **$n$-dimensional array** is a [[Function|function]] $\v A \colon \{1 \bidot d_1\} \times \dots \times \{1 \bidot d_n\} \to S$.

## Notation

- The statement $\v A \colon \{1 \bidot d_1\} \times \dots \times \{1 \bidot d_n\} \to S$ is also denoted as $\v A \in S^{d_1 \times \dots \times d_n}$.
