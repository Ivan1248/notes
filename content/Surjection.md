Let $f\colon X \to Y$ be a [[Function|function]].

$f$ is a **surjection** onto $Y'$ iff $\operatorname{im}(f)=Y'$.

## Notes

Synonymous phrases for _surjection onto $Y'$_:
- surjective onto $Y'$,
- onto $Y'$.
