An **undirected graph** is a [[Graph|graph]] $(V, E)$ where $E$ is a [[Symmetric relation|symmetric relation]].

## Notes

- A different definition. An **undirected graph** is an [[Ordered pair|pair]] $(V, E)$ where $V$ is a [[Set|set]], and $E\subseteq\{\{x,y\}:x,y\in V\}$. Note that $\{x,x\} = \{x\}$.
