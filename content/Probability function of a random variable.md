The **probability function of** a [[Random variable|random variable]] $\rvar x\colon\Omega\to X$ on a [[Probability space|probability space]] $S=(\Omega,\Sigma,P)$ is a [[Function|function]] $\P_{\rvar x,S}\colon X\to[0\bidot 1]$ such that $\forall x\in X: \P_{\rvar x,S}(x) = P(\rvar x = x)$.  

## Notation

$\P[\rvar x]$ denotes the probability function of $\rvar x$.

## Notes

For a random variable $\rvar x\colon \Omega\to X$ with a [[Countable set|countable]] [[Support of a random variable|support]] $S\subseteq X$ and [[Distribution of a random variable|distribution]] $\mathit\Pi_{\rvar x}$: $$  
\forall X'\subseteq X: \mathit\Pi_{\rvar x}(X')=\sum_{x \in X'\cap S} \P(\rvar x=x) \text.
$$
