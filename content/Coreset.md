Let $L$ be a [[Machine learning algorithm|machine learning algorithm]].  
Let $D$ be [[Training data|training data]] for $L$.  
Let $E$ be a [[Machine learning evaluation|evaluation]] of $L$.

## Related

The $n$-coreset (coreset of size $n$) of $(L, D, E)$ is a set from $$  
\argmax_{S : S\subseteq D \land \lvert S\rvert = n} E(L(D)) \text.
$$
