A **unit complex number** is a [[Complex numbers|complex number]] $z\in\C$ with [[Magnitude of a complex number|magnitude]] $\lvert z\rvert = 1$.

## Notes

- A unit complex number can be expressed as $\cos(\theta) + \i \sin(\theta)$
- Multiplication with a unit complex number $\cos(\theta)+\i\sin(\theta)$ rotates around the origin by $\theta$.
- [[Euler's formula]] holds.
