Synonyms: loop-graph, loop graph, directed graph, digraph, loop-digraph.

A **graph** is a [[Ordered pair|pair]] $G=(V, E)$ where
- $V$ (**set of vertices**) is a [[Set|set]], and
- $E$ (**set of edges**) is a [[Relation|binary relation]] on $V$.

A **vertex** of $G$ is an element of $V$.

An **edge** of $G$ is an element of $E$.

## Notes

- Some sources use _loop-graph_ instead of graph, and use _graph_ instead of [[Simple graph|simple graph]].
- Some sources use _loop-digraph_ instead of directed graph, and use _directed graph_ instead of [[Simple directed graph|simple directed graph]].
