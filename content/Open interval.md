An **open (real) interval** is a [[Set|set]] $I\subseteq \R$ such that
$$
\exists a,b\in\R\colon \forall x\in I\colon a<x<b .
$$

## Notation

- $(a\bidot b)$ denotes $\{x: x\in\R, a<x<b\}$.
