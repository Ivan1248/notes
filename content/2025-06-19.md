[\[1408.3060\] Fastfood: Approximate Kernel Expansions in Loglinear Time](https://arxiv.org/abs/1408.3060)
> Specifically, Fastfood requires O(n log d) time and O(n) storage to compute n non-linear basis functions in d dimensions, a significant improvement from O(nd) computation and storage, without sacrificing accuracy.
