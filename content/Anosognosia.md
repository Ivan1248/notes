**Anosognosia** is a neurological condition in which the patient is unaware of their neurological deficit or psychiatric condition.

## Notes
- Some anosognosia examples:
	- [Anton–Babinski syndrome](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anton%E2%80%93Babinski_syndrome "Anton–Babinski syndrome"),
	- Inverse Anton-Babinsky syndrome,
	- [Cotard delusion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cotard_delusion "Cotard delusion"),
