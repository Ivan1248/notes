The **perplexity** of a [[Probability|probability]] $p\in[0\bidot 1]$ is
$$
\operatorname{PPP}(p) = \exp(-\ln(p)) = \frac{1}{p} \text.
$$

## Notes

Perplexity can be considered an approximation of the number of possibilities assuming all possibilities have approximately the same probability.
