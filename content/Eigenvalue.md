Let $X$ and $Y$ be [[Vector space|vector spaces]] over a [[Field (Math)|field]] $R$.

An **eigenvalue** of a [[Linear mapping|linear mapping]] $A\colon X\to Y$ is $\lambda \in R\setminus\{0_R\}$ such that $\exists \v x \in X\setminus\{\cvec 0_{X}\}: A(\v x) = \lambda \v x$.
