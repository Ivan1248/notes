### 2024-04-13
- [Why Railroads Don't Need Expansion Joints - YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zqmOSMAtadc). To prevent bending of rails due expansion due to high temperatures, rails are often built under high temperatures (e.g. 40 C). Hence, when the outside temperature is lower, the rails experience tensile stress, and not compressive stress.
