Let $(\Omega, d)$ be a [[Metric space|metric space]].

A **contraction** is a function $f\colon \Omega\to\Omega$ such that there exists a  
$$\exists k\in(0\bidot 1]\colon d(f(x), f(y)) \leq k d(x, y) \text.$$
