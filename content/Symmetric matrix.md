A [[Square matrix|square matrix]] $\v A\in \R^d$ is **symmetric** iff $\forall (i,j) \in \{1\bidot d\}^2 : \v A[i, j] = \v A[j,i]$.

## Equivalent definition

A [[Square matrix|square matrix]] $\v A\in \R^d$ is **symmetric** iff $\v A$ is a [[Symmetric function|symmetric function]].
