**Valence** of a [[Phenomenal consciousness|subjective experience]] is _how good/bad_ the experience is.

## Notes

- Another way to say that a valence of a subjective is better is that it is more **positive**.
- Another way to say that a valence of a subjective is worse is that it is more **negative**.
