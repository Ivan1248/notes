In [[Action-based ethical framework]].

Let $a\colon P\to A$ be an [[Agent (normative ethics)|agent]].

A **perception history** is a tuple $(T, p)$, where
- $T$ (set of **times**) is a set such that $T\subseteq \N$ or $T \subseteq \R$,
- $p\colon T\to P$.

The **history of an agent** $a$ under perception history $(T, p)$ is the set $\{(p(t), a(p(t))) : t\in T\}$.

## Notes

- We say that at each **time** $t\in T$, an agent **receives a perception** from $P$ and **performs an action** based on the perception.
