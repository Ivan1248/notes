Let $(\Omega, d)$ be a [[Metric space|metric space]].

A **Borel σ-algebra** is the smallest [[σ-algebra]] on $\Omega$ that contains all open subsets of $\Omega$:  
$$
\operatorname B(\Omega) = \{U\subseteq \Omega: \forall u\in U\colon \exists \epsilon\in\R_{>0}\colon \{\omega\in \Omega: d(\omega, u)<\epsilon\}\subseteq U\}
$$
