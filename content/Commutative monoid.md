A **commutative monoid** is an [[Monoid|monoid]] $(K, \circ)$ such that $\circ$ is [[Commutative operation|commutative]].

## Equivalent definition

A **commutative monoid** is an [[Algebraic structure|algebraic structure]] $(K, \circ)$ such that:
- $\circ\colon K\times K\to K$ is an [[Associative operation|associative]] and [[Commutative operation|commutative]] [[Binary operation|binary operation]] such that
- there is an element $e \in K$ such that $\forall k\in K : k \circ e = k$ (**identity element**).
