Let $f\colon X\to Y$ be a [[Function|mapping]].  
Let $X'$ and $Y'$ be sets.  
Let $Z\subseteq X'\times Y'$.  

The **restriction** of $f$ to $Z$ is the [[Set operations#Intersection|intersection]] $f \cap Z$.

## Domain restriction

The **restriction** of $f$ **to domain** $X'$ is
$$
f{\restriction_{X'}} =\left\{(x,y) \in f: x\in X'\right\} .
$$
