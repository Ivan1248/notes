An **closed (real) interval** is a [[Set|set]] $I\subseteq \R$ such that
$$
\exists a,b\in\R\colon \forall x\in I\colon a\leq x\leq b .
$$

## Notation

- $[a\bidot b]$ denotes $\{x: x\in\R, a\leq x\leq b\}$.
