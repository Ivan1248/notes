Epistemic Occam's razor: _more-simply describable things_ is _more likely_.

Ontological Occam's razor: _less things_ is _more likely_.

Alternative Ontological Occam's razor: _less kinds of things_ is _more likely_.

In a _multiverse_, there is _more things_, but it is _more simply describable_.
