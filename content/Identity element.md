## Notes

Let $(S,\circ)$ be an [[Algebraic structure|algebraic structure]] that has an identity element $e\in S$. Then, $e$ is the only identity element of $S$. If $e_1$ and $e_2$ are identity elements of $S$, then $e_1 = e_1\circ e_2 = e_2$.
