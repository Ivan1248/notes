A **doubly stochastic matrix** is a [[Square matrix|square matrix]] $\v A\in\R_{\geq 0}^{d\times d}$ such that
$$
\forall \vec A' \in \{\v A, \v A^\transpose\}, i\in\{1\bidot d\} : \sum_{j\in\{1\bidot d\}} \v A'[i, j] = 1 \text.
$$
