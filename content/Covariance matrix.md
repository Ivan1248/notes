The **covariance matrix** of a [[Real random vector|random vector]] $\rvec x\colon \Omega\to \R^n$ is the matrix $\Cov(\rvar x)$ such that
$$
\forall i, j\in\{1\bidot n\} : \Cov(\rvar x)[i,j] = \Cov(\rvar x[i], \rvar x[j]) \text,
$$
where the right side of the equation is [[Covariance|covariance]].
