Let $L$ be a [[Formal language|language]] over $A$.

$L$ is **decidable** iff there exists a [[Turing machine]] $M$ such that for all $x\in A^*$:
- if $x\in L$, $M(x)$ accepts,
- if $x\not\in L$, $M(x)$ rejects.
