A **simple graph** is a [[Graph|graph]] $(V, E)$ where $V$ is an [[Anti-reflexive relation|anti-reflexive relation]].

## Notes

- Some sources use _loop-graph_ instead of [[Graph|graph]], and use _graph_ instead of simple graph.
- Some sources use _loop-digraph_ instead of [[Directed graph|directed graph]], and use _directed graph_ instead of Simple directed graph|simple directed graph.
