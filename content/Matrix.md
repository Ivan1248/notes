Let $S$ be a [[set|set]].  
Let $m,n\in\N_{\geq 1}$.

A **matrix** is an [[Array|array]] $\v A\in S^{m\times n}$.

## Alternative definition

A **matrix** is a [[function]] $\v A\colon\{1\bidot m\}\times\{1\bidot n\}\to S$..
