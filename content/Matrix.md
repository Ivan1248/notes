Let $S$ be a [[Set|set]].  
Let $m,n\in\N_{\geq 1}$.

A **matrix** is an [[Array|array]] $\v A\in S^{m\times n}$.

## Equivalent definition

A **matrix** is a [[Function|function]] $\v A\colon\{1\bidot m\}\times\{1\bidot n\}\to S$..
