Let $D$ be a [[Set|set]].

A [[Square matrix|square matrix]] $\v A\in \R^{n\times n}$ is **$D$-definite** iff $\forall \v x \in \R^{n}: \v x^\transpose \v A \v x \in D$.

A $D$-definite matrix is:
- positive-definite iff $D = \R_{>0}$,
- positive semi-definite iff $D = \R_{\geq 0}$,
- negative-definite iff $D = \R_{<0}$,
- negative semi-definite iff $D = \R_{\leq 0}$.
