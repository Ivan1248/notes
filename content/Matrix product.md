The **matrix product** of [[Matrix|matrices]] $\v A\in\R^{m\times n}$ and $\v B\in \R^{n\times p}$ is a matrix $\v C\in\R^{m\times p}$ such that $$  
\begin{align}  
\forall (i, k)\in\{1\bidot m\}\times\{1\bidot p\} : \\  
\v C[i,k] = \sum_{j=1}^n \v A[i,j] B[j,k] \text.
\end{align}
$$
